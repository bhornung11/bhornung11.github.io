Haikus

 

Anamnesis

Stirred echo of steps.
We rose. I move. They arrived.
The incessant wind.

 

 

Pebbles

 

Opal

Closed eyed galaxies.
Cover of darkness again.
Its light's just as old.

 

Amber

Shine rounded at sea.
Movements trapped in stubborn gleam.
The sun keeps playing.

 

Flint

Continents to mounds.
Finally, you have risen.
Millions of lives.

 

River Nene

Trifled kings, empires.
Land layered, flowing beyond.
Who measures the time?

 

 

Seashore

Tanned breath of the sand.
Sky wanders beyond the cliffs.
Waves under your skin.

 

 

Mountain Weekend I.

Counting contours, miles.
Ridge, valley carry legs up.
Endless views unframed.

 

 

Mountain Weekend II.

The planned excitement.
Peaks afresh all hours conquered.
Trust'd discovery.
